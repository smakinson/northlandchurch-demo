Serving and Volunteer Opportunities

Serve November 13, 2016

Community Resource Network (CRN)

Northland is partnering with Community Resource Network in an effort to mobilize faith communities in Central Florida to respond to families experiencing homelessness. There are several ways to serve and we need your help! Just a couple of hours of your time can make a big impact on families facing homelessness right here in our community. Whether you want to work directly with families or prefer to support from behind the scenes, there’s a place for you! Interested or want to learn more? Sign up!

Volunteer

Serve April 25, 2016

Volunteer, Children’s Ministries

If you have a heart for children, we want to partner with you in teaching the next generation about God! You can help build a solid foundation based on God’s Word in the lives of our children. We need teachers, classroom helpers, greeters, check-in assistants, worship team singers, puppeteers and storytellers. You don't need any previous experience – all training and materials will be provided!

Bookstore Resource Associate

Join our team in equipping those who desire to learn and grow in Christ by volunteering in the Bookstore. We need team members some weekdays and Saturday nights to assist customers, shelve books, do inventory and help with everyday duties and bookkeeping.
